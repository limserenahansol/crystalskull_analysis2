# Suggested figure legend text (copy-paste)

Use **Figure 5E/F** or **fig2 panels** depending on your manuscript layout.

## Synchrony / fraction active (`fig2_population_activity.png`, panel 3)

**Short:**  
Distribution of the per–1 s bin fraction of neurons with z-scored activity > 2 in at least one frame within the bin. Vertical lines: mean across bins. Brackets in title: 95% bootstrap CI for that mean (1000 resamples of bins). Histograms: probability density. Wilcoxon rank-sum test on per-bin fractions (Baseline vs Injection): *p* = [from panel title].

## Population mean event rate (`fig2_population_activity.png`, panel 4)

**Short:**  
Mean per-neuron event rate (z > 2 upward crossings per second). Error bars: 95% bootstrap CI of the mean across neurons (1000 resamples). *p*: two-sided Wilcoxon rank-sum test on per-neuron rates.

## Wording

- **“Synchrony”** is fine if **defined** as above; it is not millisecond-scale pairwise synchrony.
- Optionally say **“GCaMP-derived event rate”** instead of “Ca²⁺ event rate” for strict reviewers.
